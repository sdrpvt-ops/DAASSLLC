Company Overview

D.A. & Associates Consulting and Management Services LLC is a Florida limited liability company established to provide management consulting, business advisory, project support, and operational development services to clients in Florida and across the United States.

The company supports businesses that require experienced management input, practical systems development, commercial structuring, and hands-on execution support during formation, growth, restructuring, or expansion phases.

Business Activities

Management Consulting

Interim and long-term management support for early-stage and developing businesses, including operational planning, process improvement, business development, and management oversight.

Business Systems & Frameworks

Development of practical commercial, operational, reporting, and management frameworks to help businesses move from concept stage into structured operating companies.

Asset-Related Consulting

Specialist consulting support for asset management, leasing, and related business models, including early-stage business engineering and operational planning.

Project & Commercial Support

Support for client projects including planning, coordination, contractor engagement, financial modelling, documentation, and execution support.
